Get the idea.

Why are you looking at a website of an 22 year old futute engineer, you might wonder.

First of all i would really like to thank you for reading my personal website. I created this website as a part of my Production Engineering course at HSU. My goal is to show what i have learned not only in this course but also in the whole study so far. I will try to update this website regulary with new content and projects.

As a final result i would like to have an complete collection of every major academic achievement. It is a great way to show to what effort over several years can cunclude, hopefully a master of science.
