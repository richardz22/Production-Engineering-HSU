Week Three

Theory of Production Engineering

Lets try to wrap a whole module of mechanical engineering in two sessions

Of course we can not just work without any context. But unfortunately we also do not have time to do another module, so what is the compromise? Dr. Redlich suggested two lessons for us to get an overview of the topic. It is important to mention that the prurpose of this was not to learn every aspect like our colleagues, it is to get a general understanding of the topic. I guess this fits pretty good the Engineering Science.

Guess what happended, 0ver 100 pages of PowerPoint. But hold on, I also thought it was going to be exhausting. Dr. Redlich presented with such enthusiasm that it got me really hooked. It was easy to stay interested and to follow him, yet of course it was an total information overflow.

Topics of the first session

  • Casting
    • Sand Casting
    • Permanent mold and die casting
    • Investment casting
    • Lost foam casting
    • Centrifugal casting
  • Plastic Technology
    • Extrusion
    • Calendering
    • Injection molding
    • Blow molding
    • compression molding
    • foaming
  • Sintering
